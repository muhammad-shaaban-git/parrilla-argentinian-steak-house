Experience the Passion

Located in the heart of Milton Keynes, Parrilla brings the vibrant flavors of the Argentine pampas to your plate. Specializing in high-quality cuts and traditional "asado" techniques, we pride ourselves on offering a dining experience that is both elegant and authentic.

Whether you're looking for our succulent Wagyu steaks, famous beef short ribs, or our Halal-friendly options, every dish is prepared with the utmost care to ensure a "special occasion" vibe every time you visit.

Our Offerings

Argentinian Grill

Premium Ribeye, Sirloin, and Filet steaks cooked to your preference on our traditional grill.

Signature cocktails including our famous Watermelon Cucumber Mojito and a curated wine list.
